    How to Play

    Fill the pyramid with numbers from a shuffled stack of 99 cards labeled 1–99 (no repeats). You have 60 seconds to fill in the pyramid.

    Place or replace the face-up card into any space on the pyramid from the current value shown in the flipped-over card of the stack.

    Row rules: After a placement, that row’s total must be less than or equal to the row above, and greater than or equal to the row below. This check is only if the row above and/or below has any numbers.

    Placing a valid number: +3 seconds to your time, and the next card in the stack is revealed.

    Placing an invalid number: −5 seconds to your time. You can still try and play the current card, or pass.

    Pass skips the current card and reveals the next one in the stack. Once you pass on a card, you can't use it again.

    To WIN: Fill all 10 spaces. Your score will be equal to all values in the pyramid, + 5 points per second left on the clock, + 1 point per card left in the stack.

    Time’s up, out of cards, or not enough cards left to fill the remaining spaces: Your score is half of the pyramid total.
